As a designer, I prefer to think in terms of People Experience because we are all more than just users pushing buttons. We are complex beings with needs, desires and passions. Good design helps people achieve their goals and feel great about it.

I enjoy working with tech companies to build engaging and satisfying user experiences. Most of my work has been focused on designin web-based apps, optimizing their user flows, drafting personas and improving their features based on data.

I've worked in Argentina, Poland, Colombia and Brazil. As a result, I've developed a passion for languages and cultures. I'm fluent in French, Portuguese, English and Spanish but I also know some Italian, German and Polish.
