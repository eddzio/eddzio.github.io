Hi, I'm Eduardo. I’m a designer who has worked with and co-founded different startups around the world.

I’ve been part of international teams in Argentina (Hoojobs, Keepcon) and Canada (LaCucaracha) that create delightful digital experiences based on research and testing. In the last years I’ve specialized in web-based apps but my interests are now shifting towards mobile experiences.

When I’m not designing I’m usually organizing design events for the Barranquilla Design Group or offering help to local startups. My other non design-related activities involve reading, writing and playing guitar.
